Installation
1
Go to GHL Agency Settings → Company → Custom JS
2
Paste this script tag and click Save:
<script src="https://cdn.thecliqlabs.com/loader.js"></script>
3
Refresh GHL — CliqLabs will appear in your sidebar instantly!
4
Come back here, pick your theme, click Save & Apply — done! ✅
