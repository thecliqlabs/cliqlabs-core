2
Go to GHL Agency Settings → Company → Custom JS — paste the JS code from Step 3 above.
3
Click Save Changes then hard refresh GHL with Ctrl+Shift+R (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac).
4
✅ Your GHL is now branded! The ⚡ CliqLabs menu item will appear in your sidebar. Come back here anytime to change your theme.
